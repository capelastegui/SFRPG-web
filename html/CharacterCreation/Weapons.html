Weapon Keywords
  • Versatile: +1 damage per W if wielder has a free off-hand.
  • Recharge: -2 to hit unless wielder has spent a move action to recharge since their last attack.
  • Recharge (move action). Move 1 square. You can ignore the Recharge penalty to hit with one of your weapons for your next attack power with that weapon..
  • Parry: +1 AC vs melee attacks. Does not stack multiple times.
