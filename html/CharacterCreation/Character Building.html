Process:

  • Choose Class
    • Choose class build (determines Primary, Secondary abilities)
  • Choose Race
  • Choose Abilities
    • Choose array type: Flexible or Focused
    • Choose Primary and Secondary abilities (see class, subclass)
    • Choose Weak ability
  • Choose Skills
    • Determine skill list: class skills, race skills
    • Determine bonus skills from favored racial abilities (+1 skill for each Primary/Secondary ability matching a racial ability)
    • Choose skills
  • Choose Gear
    • Choose weapons, implements
    • Choose armor
  • Choose Feats
    • Default: Weapon Focus (chosen weapon/implement)
  • Choose Powers
  • Calculate stats
