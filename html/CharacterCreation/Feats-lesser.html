Multiclass Implement

Repeatable: No - Level 1

Keywords: Multiclass

Requires: A Multiclass Feat.
Multiclass to a class with Implement attacks.

Choose an Implement that your Multiclass has Training with. Gain Training with that Implement.
Choose a skill from your Multiclass class skill list in which you are untrained. You gain training in that skill.

Multiclass Learning: At-Will

Repeatable: No - Level 1

Keywords: Multiclass

Requires: A Multiclass Feat

Choose a Level 1 At-Will Attack Power from your Multiclass. You can use that power once per encounter.
If the Primary Ability for that power does not match yours, use an ability value of 4 instead.
If the Secondary Ability for that power does not match yours, use an ability value of 2 instead.

Multiclass Learning: Daily

Repeatable: No - Level 1

Keywords: Multiclass

Requires: A Multiclass Feat

Choose a Daily Attack Power from your Class. You lose that power, and gain a Daily Attack Power of the same level or lower from your Multiclass.
If the Primary Ability for that power does not match yours, use an ability value of 4 instead.
If the Secondary Ability for that power does not match yours, use an ability value of 2 instead.

Multiclass Learning: Encounter

Repeatable: No - Level 1

Keywords: Multiclass

Requires: A Multiclass Feat

Choose an Encounter Attack Power from your Class. You lose that power, and gain an Encounter Attack Power of the same level or lower from your Multiclass.
If the Primary Ability for that power does not match yours, use an ability value of 4 instead.
If the Secondary Ability for that power does not match yours, use an ability value of 2 instead.

Multiclass Learning: Utility

Repeatable: No - Level 1

Keywords: Multiclass

Requires: A Multiclass Feat

Choose a Utility Power from your Class. You lose that power, and gain a Utility Power of the same level or lower from your Multiclass.
