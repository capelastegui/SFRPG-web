Combat Concepts

  • Miss damage:
    • stacks, caps at hit damage
  • Crit range
    • Base d20 roll + crit range modifier over 20 - crit
  • Critical hit: Double damage on a natural hit roll of 20 (or less, with extra Crit Range)
  • High Crit:
    • On a crit with a weapon attack, +5 damage per W.
    • On a crit with an implement attack, +5 damage per die.
  • Pierce Resist:
    • Pierce resist (type) halves the resisted damage from resistances to a given type.
  • Burst/Blast changes
    • -2 penalty to hit with Area attack if origin square is in Cover or Concealment
    • All creatures not in the origin square grant Cover for burst and blast attacks.
    • Cannot have origin square of Area attack with Superior Cover or Total Concealment
    • New design guidelines:
      • Multi-target powers gain a -2 penalty to hit unless:
        • Very low damage
        • Sacrifice Miss Damage
        • Pure damage power with no side effect, and reasonable damage, number of targets.
      • New combination?: Area blast. Cone from far square.






Combat Maneuvers

  • Draw weapon
  • Draw (move action). Sheathe or store any number of weapons or objects from your hands. Draw, or pick up from the ground, up to one weapon or object per hand. Move 1 square.
  • Quick Draw (minor action). Drop any number of weapons or objects from your hands. Draw up to one weapon or object per hand. You gain a -2 penalty to attack rolls this turn.
  • Drink Potion
  • Drink Potion (minor action). Drink a potion you have stored or in your hand.
  • Shifting Maneuver
  • Shifting Maneuver (move action). Shift 1 square. You gain a -2 penalty to attack rolls this turn.
  • Note: this replaces the Shift combat maneuver.


Implement basic attack
A player character may pick an implement basic attack if they meet the following conditions:
Proficiency with an implement
an Implement attack power
A character can only have one implement basic attack.
When picking an implement basic attack, the player chooses a damage type shared from one of their implement attack powers. That is the damage type for the implement basic attack. The player then chooses a defense targeted by that implement attack power. That is the defense targeted by the implement basic attack
